Hailing from the vibrant underground scene, TRIOT is a three-piece punk rock force to be reckoned with.
Formed in 2016, Ohad Gadassi (vocals/guitar), Ofek Shlinger (bass), and Adar Ohayon (drums) channel the electrifying energy of Green Day, the furious riffs of Avenged Sevenfold, and the infectious pop-punk hooks of Sum 41.
With their debut EP, "MTV," already making waves, TRIOT isn't slowing down.
Their self-titled album is on the horizon, primed to unleash a potent cocktail of raw energy and anthemic singalongs.
